## Combat Rotation, Combo Flow, and Transformation Timing

A clean Anti Magic loop is less about pressing all buttons and more about role order.

### Reliable neutral-to-burst sequence

1. **Open with low-commitment pressure** (starter/poke skill).
2. **Confirm contact** before committing to heavy burst.
3. **Use extension skill** only after you secure positional advantage.
4. **Enter transformation** when at least two high-value tools are ready.
5. **Spend finisher** to close damage cycle, then disengage/reset.

| Phase | Goal | What to Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| **Neutral** | Probe, bait reactions | Burning high cooldowns on first contact |
| **Confirm** | Lock opponent/mob in place | Overextending without backup skill |
| **Burst** | Max damage chain | Mashing all skills without order |
| **Reset** | Reposition, recover cooldowns | Staying in melee with empty kit |

In PvE, prioritize consistency and route density (clear speed over risky style points).  
In PvP, prioritize confirm quality and punish windows (accuracy over volume).

If your current **clover origins anti magic stats** setup feels weak, test rotation quality first:
- Are you missing openers?
- Are you transforming too early?
- Are you spending your finisher before setup is complete?

Small sequencing fixes often outperform big stat changes.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best clover origins anti magic stats for beginners in 2026?**

A: Start with a balanced approach: strong damage investment with enough survivability to stay in fights. New players usually perform better when they can survive combo mistakes while learning timing.

**Q: Should I focus on PvE or PvP when building Anti Magic first?**

A: PvE-first progression is usually smoother because it funds gear and upgrades faster. Once your core kit is stable, make a second preset for PvP with higher utility and catch potential.

**Q: Why does my transformation feel weak even with good stats?**

A: Most often it’s a sequence issue. Enter transformation after a confirmed hit and with multiple high-value skills available. If you transform during neutral or with empty cooldowns, damage drops sharply.

**Q: How often should I respec my clover origins anti magic stats?**

A: Respec when your content changes (story grind, boss farm, arena) or after major gear milestones. Frequent small adjustments based on performance are better than one rigid “final” build.
