For competitive fights, **clover origins anti magic transformation** is about tempo. You win neutral with restraint, then cash out with transformed pressure.

### Neutral Phase Rules

### Punish Window Rules
- Confirm one reliable hit before full chain commitment.
- Transform after you verify the enemy cannot escape cleanly.

### Closeout Rules
- If enemy survives burst, switch to safe pressure.

- Don’t tunnel vision if your transformation timer is ending.

| PvP Situation | Common Mistake | Better Adjustment |
