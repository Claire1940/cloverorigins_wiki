## Optimal Stat Distribution for Fire Users

To maximize the effectiveness of **clover origins fire magic**, you must allocate your stat points wisely. Unlike physical builds that rely on Strength, Fire Magic scales purely off your Magic and Stamina pools. In the 2026 update, balancing these stats is more important than ever due to the increased mana costs of high-tier spells.

### Priority 1: Magic Power
This is your primary stat. It increases the initial hit damage of your fireballs and directly boosts the tick damage of your Burn status effect.

### Priority 2: Stamina/Mana
Fire spells are "hungry." Without a deep mana pool, you will find yourself unable to cast your ultimate abilities during long boss fights. Aim for a 2:1 ratio between Magic and Stamina during your early levels.

### Priority 3: Defense
While Fire is an offensive powerhouse, you cannot deal damage if you are dead. Investing a small portion of points into Defense or Health ensures you can survive a combo from a Light or Dark magic user in PVP.

## PVP Tactics and Combos

In the arena, **clover origins fire magic** users are often feared for their "pressure" playstyle. You don't necessarily need to land a single massive hit; you need to keep the opponent burning and panicking.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid staying stationary. Fire magic casters are vulnerable to "Rush" builds. Always keep your Phoenix Flight off cooldown for a quick getaway.

### The "Searing Pressure" Combo
A common effective combo involves using Fire Pillar to knock the opponent into the air, followed immediately by a Fireball volley. As they land, use Flame Burst to knock them back and apply a fresh stack of Burn. This sequence maximizes damage while keeping you at a safe distance.

When fighting against Water magic users, be cautious. Water spells can sometimes extinguish certain environmental fire effects or provide shields that mitigate DoT. Against Wind users, focus on timing your Fire Pillar, as their high mobility makes them difficult to hit with standard projectiles.
