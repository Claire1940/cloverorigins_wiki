## Advanced Combat Strategies

Mastering **clover origins water magic** involves more than just clicking buttons. You must understand the "Zone Control" philosophy. Your goal is to dictate where the enemy can and cannot move.

### PvE Strategy: Dungeon Clearing
In dungeons, use **Whirlpool Trap** to gather mobs into a tight cluster. Once they are grouped, follow up immediately with **Crashing Wave**. This combo maximizes your AoE damage and keeps the enemies stunned, preventing them from damaging your teammates. If a boss prepares a heavy attack, drop your **Aqua Wall** to mitigate the damage for the entire party.

### PvP Strategy: The Kiting Method
In player-versus-player combat, the Water mage's greatest strength is "kiting"—the act of dealing damage while moving away from the opponent. Use **Water Ball** to poke at the enemy from a distance. If they use a dash or mobility skill to reach you, use **Crashing Wave** to reset the distance. 

*   **Step 1:** Maintain a mid-range distance.
*   **Step 2:** Use **Whirlpool Trap** to force the enemy to use their dodge/stamina.
*   **Step 3:** Once their stamina is low, hit them with the **Hydro Beam** ultimate for a guaranteed kill.
