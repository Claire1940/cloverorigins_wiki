## Light Magic: The Speed King

Light Magic is frequently cited as the **clover origins best magic type** for players who value mobility above all else. Known as the fastest magic attribute in existence, it allows users to zip across the battlefield, making them nearly impossible to target for slower mages.

### Key Abilities and Strengths
Light Magic users like Lumiere and Patri demonstrate that this attribute isn't just about running away; it's about overwhelming the opponent with a barrage of high-speed projectiles. Spells like "Arrows of Judgment" can rain down hundreds of light blades, covering a massive area and dealing significant chip damage that adds up quickly.

*   **Movement:** Light Magic provides the fastest travel spells in the game, essential for dodging ultimates.
*   **Healing:** Unlike many offensive types, Light Magic often includes a "Healing Ray" spell, allowing for self-sustain during prolonged fights.
*   **Range:** Most spells are long-range, allowing you to pressure opponents from a safe distance.

## Dark Magic: The Ultimate Counter

If Light Magic is the epitome of speed, Dark Magic is its polar opposite. While naturally slower, Dark Magic possesses unique properties that make it a hard counter to almost every other magic type in the game. It is the only attribute capable of absorbing incoming spells and even cutting through spatial dimensions.

### Combat Mechanics
Dark Magic users, inspired by Captain Yami, typically augment their magical power with physical weapons like katanas. This hybrid approach compensates for the magic's inherent lack of speed. By coating a blade in darkness, the user can deliver devastating slashes that ignore traditional magical defenses.

| Spell Name | Effect | Best Used For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Black Hole** | Absorbs incoming magical projectiles | Defensive Neutralization |
| **Black Cocoon** | Creates a protective spherical barrier | Survival / Transport |
| **Dimension Slash** | A massive strike that ignores durability | Finishing Moves |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Dark Magic requires high skill expression. If you miss your "Black Hole" timing, you are vulnerable to faster attributes like Light or Wind.

## Kotodama Magic: Breaking Reality

For those lucky enough to roll a Mythical attribute, Kotodama (Word Soul) Magic is arguably the **clover origins best magic type** for pure, unadulterated power. This magic allows the user to manifest physical objects and phenomena simply by "speaking" them into existence.

In the 2026 meta, Kotodama Magic stands out because many of its manifestations are considered "physical" rather than "magical," meaning they can bypass certain magic-resistance buffs. The ability to summon iron spikes, walls of fire, or even vacuum pockets makes the Kotodama user a one-man army.

### Why Kotodama is Overpowered
1.  **Versatility:** It can mimic the effects of multiple other magic types.
2.  **Crowd Control:** The "Silence" or "Bind" commands can render an opponent helpless for several seconds.
3.  **Summoning:** Users can create monsters to fight alongside them, shifting the numbers advantage in PvP.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the clover origins best magic type for a solo player?**

A: For solo players, **Light Magic** is often considered the best choice. Its combination of high speed for dodging and self-healing capabilities allows you to tackle difficult bosses and escape unfavorable PvP encounters without needing a dedicated support mage.

**Q: Is Anti-Magic better than Kotodama Magic?**

A: It depends on the matchup. Anti-Magic is a "Unique" tier that specifically counters magic users. In a 1v1 against a mage, Anti-Magic is superior. However, Kotodama Magic has better area-of-effect (AoE) and versatility for clearing waves of enemies or fighting multiple opponents at once.

**Q: How often should I re-roll my magic attribute?**

A: You should only re-roll if you are stuck with a Common or Rare attribute. Once you land a Legendary attribute like Dark, Time, or Light, it is generally better to keep it and master it rather than chasing a Mythical roll, which has a very low success rate.

**Q: Can I have more than one magic type in Clover Origins?**

A: Normally, players are locked to one magic attribute per character. However, certain rare items or specific game events in 2026 may allow for temporary "Dual-Casting" or hybrid abilities, though these are typically reserved for end-game players.
