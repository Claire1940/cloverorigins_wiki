### S-Tier: The Meta-Definers
**Anti-Magic** remains the undisputed king of the **clover origins tier list**. Its ability to transform the user and nullify incoming magical threats makes it a necessity for high-level raids. However, for those focused on leveling, **Earth Magic** is arguably more valuable. As seen in recent high-level gameplay, the AoE (Area of Effect) on Earth spells is "insane," allowing players to gather entire groups of bandits or goblins and dispatch them in a single rotation.

### A-Tier: High-Performance Grimoires
**Lightning Magic** is the go-to for players who value speed. It offers a "Flash Step" style of combat that makes dodging boss telegraphs much easier. While it lacks the massive spread of Earth, its focused damage is superior for taking down bosses like the Goblin King. **Fire Magic** follows closely, providing consistent burn damage that scales well into the mid-to-late game (levels 450-750).

> **💡 Tip:** If you roll Lightning or Earth early on, do not risk re-rolling unless you have at least 50+ spins saved up. These are top-tier options for reaching max level.

## Leveling Strategy and Quest Progression

To reach the max level in 2026, you must follow a strict questing path. The game is designed around "stat-checking," meaning if your level is too low for a zone, you will struggle regardless of your placement on the **clover origins tier list**.

### The Path to Max Level
1. **Levels 1-150:** Focus on the starting Bandit quests. Use any grimoire you get initially (usually Water).
2. **Levels 150-450:** Move to the Grimoire Tower and roll for an Epic tier magic. This is where Lightning or Earth becomes vital.
3. **Levels 450-750:** Grinding becomes more intensive. This is the "Goblin Era." Focus on mob grouping.
4. **Levels 750-Max:** Boss farming and unlocking Dash V2.

### Unlocking Dash V2
Dash V2 is a mandatory upgrade for any serious player. Unlike the standard dash, V2 functions like a "Flash Step," covering a significantly larger distance and providing i-frames (invincibility frames). To unlock this, you must find the **Sung-Jinwoo NPC** and have used your standard dash at least 100 times. It is a small investment for a massive increase in survivability.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some quests have NPCs that move around frequently. Use Earth magic's ground-slam abilities to pin them down and prevent them from resetting their leash distance.

## Mastery and Transformations

As you progress toward the level cap, you will unlock Mastery points. These should be invested based on your current placement in the **clover origins tier list**. If you are using a high-AoE magic like Earth, focus on **Mana Efficiency** to keep your spells active. If you are using Lightning, focus on **Cooldown Reduction** to maintain your mobility.

The ultimate goal for many is the **Anti-Magic Transformation**. This is a late-game unlock that requires specific quest completion and a high level of mastery. Once activated, it temporarily boosts all stats and allows you to bypass the magical defenses of the game's hardest bosses.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best magic for beginners in the clover origins tier list?**

A: Earth Magic is currently the best for beginners. Its massive Area of Effect (AoE) allows you to clear early-game mob quests much faster than single-target magics like Wind or Water.

**Q: How do I get more Grimoire spins?**

A: You can obtain spins by completing daily quests, defeating world bosses like the Goblin King, or purchasing them with in-game currency at the Grimoire Tower.

**Q: Is Dash V2 worth the grind?**

A: Absolutely. Dash V2 changes your movement from a simple hop to a long-distance teleport. It is essential for dodging high-level boss attacks and navigating the map quickly.

**Q: Can I change from Good to Evil later in the game?**

A: Yes, but it usually requires a specific reset item or a high-cost NPC interaction. It is better to choose your path early and stick to it to maximize your reputation rewards.
