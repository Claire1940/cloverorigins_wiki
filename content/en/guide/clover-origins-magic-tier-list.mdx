## Ranking Method: What Actually Matters in Real Matches

Most players mis-rank magic by focusing only on one-shot potential. That is not enough for a dependable **Clover Origins magic ranking**.

Use this weighted method instead:

| Metric | Weight | Questions to Ask |
|---|---:|---|
| **Mobility** | **30%** | Can you engage/disengage safely? Can you rotate quickly? |
| **Damage Reliability** | **25%** | How easy is your damage to land under pressure? |
| **Utility/Control** | **20%** | Do you have CC, spacing tools, or defensive value? |
| **Scaling & Build Flexibility** | **15%** | Does it stay useful from mid-game to late-game? |
| **Execution Difficulty** | **10%** | Is the skill floor realistic for your current level? |

This method is why Light and Time rank so highly: they perform well in multiple categories, not just one.
