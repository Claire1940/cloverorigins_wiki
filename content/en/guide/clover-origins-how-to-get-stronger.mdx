## Early Game Progression: The Path to Level 15

The initial phase of your journey is centered around rapid leveling. When you first spawn into the world, your primary objective is to reach level 15 as quickly as possible. This is the magic threshold where the game truly opens up, allowing you to move beyond basic physical combat and into the realm of specialized magic.

To start, focus on the "Bandit Beater" missions located near the starting village. These quests are designed to provide a steady stream of experience points (EXP) and Gold. Instead of simply wandering and fighting random mobs, always ensure you have an active quest from a nearby NPC. This doubles your efficiency, as you earn rewards for both the individual kills and the quest completion.

| Quest Type | Recommended Level | Primary Rewards | Efficiency Rating |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bandit Beater I** | 1 - 7 | **EXP, Coins** | High |

## Clover Origins How to Get Stronger: Mastering the Grimoire System

While it is tempting to settle for the first one you get, many veteran players suggest "rolling" for a higher-tier magic if you have the resources. Higher rarity grimoires typically offer more versatile spell kits and better damage scaling as you reach the late game.

## Optimizing Your Stat Points

Every time you level up, you are awarded stat points. Distributing these points haphazardly is a common mistake that can hinder your progress. To optimize your build, you must align your stats with your chosen magic type and playstyle. In the 2026 meta, specialized builds generally outperform "jack-of-all-trades" configurations.

### Recommended Build Paths

1.  **The Pure Mage:** Focus heavily on **Magic Power** and **Stamina**. This build maximizes the damage of your grimoire spells and allows you to cast them more frequently.
2.  **The Magic Knight:** A balanced approach between **Strength** and **Magic Power**. This is ideal for players who like to weave sword strikes in between spell cooldowns.
3.  **The Tank:** Prioritize **Defense** and **Health**. This is less common for solo play but essential for players who want to lead the charge in difficult group raids.

| Attribute | Effect | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **Magic Power** | Increases spell damage and scaling | **High** (For Casters) |
| **Strength** | Increases physical weapon damage | **High** (For Melee) |
| **Defense** | Reduces damage taken from enemies | **Medium** (All Builds) |
| **Stamina** | Increases mana pool and dash frequency | **High** (For Mobility) |

> **Warning:** Resetting stats often requires premium currency or rare in-game items. Plan your build carefully from level 1 to avoid unnecessary costs.

**Q: Does my choice of magic affect clover origins how to get stronger in the long run?**

A: Yes, your magic choice significantly impacts your growth rate. Some magic types are "fast farmers," meaning they can kill groups of enemies quickly, leading to faster leveling. However, almost any magic can be made powerful with the right stat allocation and gear.
