## Best Progression Timing for Trait Rerolls

The biggest mistake with **clover origins traits** is rerolling at the wrong time. If you reroll too early, you lose momentum. If you reroll too late, you may suffer through inefficient farming longer than needed.

Use this checkpoint system:

| Milestone | What to Do | Trait Decision |
|---|---|---|
| **Level 1–50** | Complete starter quest loop | Keep any usable roll; save rerolls |
| **Around 50** | Obtain/spin grimoire at tower NPC path | Only reroll if trait is clearly weak for your playstyle |
| **50–100** | Farm XP quickly and stabilize combat | Save spins unless farming feels too slow |
| **Post-100** | Unlock broom/flight, prep raids | Main reroll window begins |
| **Raid-ready** | Farm Licht/Yuno, dungeon entries | Push for high-value trait synergy |

This method keeps your account growth smooth while still letting you optimize **clover origins traits** before harder content.

- If you run dungeons, mobility and survivability become more important than raw peak damage.

## Efficient Farming Route That Supports Trait Optimization

To maximize value from **clover origins traits**, combine rerolls with a repeatable route. This prevents “spin and hope” gameplay and replaces it with measurable progress.

1. **Reach level 50 quickly** through early quest farming.
2. **Get your grimoire** and test your current trait in real combat.
3. **Push to level 100** without overspending rerolls.
4. **Unlock flight** via broom questline.
5. **Start raid cycle**: Licht and Yuno on spawn timers.
6. **Run dungeons** when keys are available for fragment progression.
7. **Reroll traits only after a farming session**, when you can feel actual bottlenecks.

| Activity | Target Reward | Why It Supports Trait Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Licht raid** | Orb chance, rare drop chance | Tests boss damage and survival trait value |

If your current trait feels average but your clears are stable, keep farming. If you repeatedly fail survival checks or timing checks, that’s your signal to reroll.
