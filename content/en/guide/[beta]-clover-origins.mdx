## Exploring the World of Clover Origins

The map in the current beta is divided into several distinct regions, each with its own level requirements and enemy types. Progressing through these zones in the correct order is vital for efficient leveling. If you venture into high-level areas too early, the "Level Gap" penalty will reduce the damage you deal, making combat nearly impossible.

1. **The Starting Village (Levels 1-15):** Focus on basic delivery quests and defeating low-level bandits to get a feel for your spells.
2. **The Whispering Woods (Levels 15-35):** This area introduces elemental enemies. It is a great place to farm for basic equipment drops.
3. **The Royal Capital (Levels 35-60):** Here you can join squads, participate in organized PVP, and access the mid-tier Grimoire Tower.
4. **The Forsaken Lands (Levels 60+):** Currently the highest-level zone in the beta, featuring world bosses and legendary loot.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Forsaken Lands are a "Full PVP" zone. Players can attack you freely, so it is best to travel with a squad or guild.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

Mastering combat in **[beta] clover origins** requires more than just mashing your spell keys. The game features a sophisticated "Mana Skin" mechanic and "Perfect Dodge" system that can turn the tide of a battle.

*   **Mana Skin:** By holding the guard button, you consume mana to create a shield. This is essential for surviving ultimate attacks from bosses.
*   **Perfect Dodge:** Timing your dash exactly as an enemy attack lands will trigger a brief slow-motion effect, allowing you to counter-attack with increased damage.
*   **Spell Canceling:** Some spells can be canceled halfway through their animation to bait an opponent's dodge, leaving them vulnerable to a follow-up strike.
