## Top Tier Magic Deep Dive

### Anti-Magic (S-Tier)
Anti-Magic remains the pinnacle of the **tier list clover origins** for 2026. It isn't just about damage; it provides a transformation that fundamentally changes how you interact with bosses. By negating magical projectiles, users can stay in the face of even the most dangerous enemies. It is currently the hardest magic to obtain but offers the highest return on investment for endgame players.

### Lightning Magic (S-Tier)
Lightning is the preferred choice for players who prioritize speed. Its ability to "Flash Step" through combat makes it nearly impossible for enemies to track in PvP. In PvE, the high critical hit rate allows you to bypass the defense of late-game bosses. If you manage to roll this at the Grimoire Tower, it is generally recommended to keep it unless you specifically need AoE.

### Earth Magic (A-Tier)
As noted by many top players, Earth magic is the "sleeper hit" of the current season. Its ability to pull multiple NPCs into a single point and crush them simultaneously makes it significantly faster for leveling than many S-tier options. If your goal is to reach max level quickly, Earth is arguably the most efficient tool in your arsenal.

### Obtaining Dash V2
To upgrade your mobility, you must locate the Sung-Jinwoo NPC. Unlike other upgrades that simply require gold or level requirements, Dash V2 requires you to prove your mastery. You must use your standard dash 100 times before the NPC will grant you the "Flash Step" upgrade. This version of the dash covers nearly triple the distance and provides brief invincibility frames.

## Leveling Strategy and Quest Progression

To make the most of your **tier list clover origins** knowledge, you need to apply it to the leveling grind. The game follows a linear progression path that scales in difficulty once you pass the level 500 mark.

1. **Levels 1-150:** Focus on the Bandit quests. Use any magic you roll; Water or Wind is sufficient here.
2. **Levels 150-450:** Move to the Grimoire Tower. Try to roll for at least an Epic tier magic like Earth or Fire.
3. **Levels 450-750:** This is the "Wall." Use Earth magic's AoE to group enemies. This is also where you should start rolling for a better Broom to travel between quest givers faster.
4. **Levels 750-Max:** Focus on Boss Quests. The Goblin Boss and the Demon Boss provide the most EXP. At this stage, high single-target damage (Lightning or Anti-Magic) becomes more valuable than AoE.

> **Warning:** Be careful when rolling for new grimoires if you already have an Epic. With only 30 rolls, you have a high chance of ending up with a Common Wind grimoire, which will significantly slow down your progress.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best magic in the tier list clover origins for beginners?**

A: Earth magic is currently the best for beginners. While it isn't the rarest, its massive Area of Effect (AoE) allows you to complete quests and level up much faster than single-target magics like Lightning or Water.

**Q: How do I get more spins for my Grimoire?**

A: You can obtain spins by completing specific milestones, such as reaching level 150, 450, and 750. Additionally, developers often release codes that provide free spins at the Grimoire Tower.

**Q: Is the Fire Broom worth the 0.1% drop rate?**

A: Yes. The Fire Broom is significantly faster than the standard broom and even the rarer Epic brooms. If you plan on reaching the max level and participating in endgame raids, the travel time saved makes it a top-tier utility item.

**Q: Where can I find the NPC for Dash V2?**

A: The Dash V2 NPC, modeled after Sung-Jinwoo, is located in the hidden grove near the level 750 quest area. Remember that you must have used your dash 100 times before he will offer you the upgrade.
