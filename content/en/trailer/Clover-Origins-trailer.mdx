## Broom Travel and Why Movement Changes Everything

One of the best signals in the **Clover Origins trailer** is how much emphasis the game puts on movement. Once broom travel enters your loop, your account stops feeling locked to one slow grind lane. Better mobility means cleaner quest turn-ins, faster boss resets, and less wasted downtime between farming pockets.

This matters even more than it sounds. A lot of early frustration in Clover Origins comes from inefficient travel, not just weak damage. If you fix movement, everything else becomes easier to manage:

- You reach the next quest target faster.
- You recover from a bad route choice more cleanly.
- You can compare grind spots without committing to a slow walk every time.
- You make better use of temporary rewards from codes, resets, or fresh grimoire rolls.

The trailer does not hand you the full route, but it clearly tells you that mobility is part of progression. Treat broom unlocks as account upgrades, not vanity items.
