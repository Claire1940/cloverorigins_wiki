## Understanding the LSA Classifications

In the current 2026 meta, creatures are categorized primarily by their threat level and biological origin. The **clover origins trello** classifies the primary antagonist as a Large-Scale Aggressor (LSA). This creature, often referred to as "Clover," is not merely a mindless beast but is characterized as a terrified, confused infant separated from its family across dimensions.

The physical design of an LSA dictates how players must interact with it. With a height ranging from 240 to 300 feet and a quadrupedal movement style, these monsters can cover city blocks in seconds. Their skin is notoriously resistant to conventional high-powered artillery, meaning players must focus on evasion rather than direct confrontation.

## The Parasite Threat: Secondary Combat Mechanics

While the massive Clover monster is the face of the game, the **clover origins trello** highlights that the secondary monsters—the parasites—are often the cause of more player deaths. These creatures reside on the LSA's skin in numbers exceeding 2,000 and drop to the ground to hunt smaller prey (humans).

These parasites move with spider-like agility, capable of jumping great distances and clinging to ceilings. Their blood contains concentrated levels of "Katy Nomitsu," a substance that causes a violent biological reaction in humans. If bitten, a player has a very limited window to find a cure before their character experiences a fatal "burst" event.

## Advanced Survival Strategies for 2026

Surviving a round in Clover Origins requires more than just hiding. As the game world evolves, players must utilize the environment and the monster's own biology against it.

### Using the Environment
The LSA's size is its greatest weakness in urban environments. Use narrow alleyways and subway tunnels where the 300-foot beast cannot reach. However, be wary of the parasites that thrive in these enclosed spaces. Always carry a thermal scanner to detect the heat signatures of parasites clinging to the ceiling.

### Managing the "Katy Nomitsu" Levels
If you are bitten by a parasite, your primary goal is to manage your internal toxicity. The **clover origins trello** lists several crafting components found in the "Tagruato" labs scattered across the map. These can be used to create a temporary stabilizer that pauses the 20-minute death timer, giving you enough time to reach an extraction point.
