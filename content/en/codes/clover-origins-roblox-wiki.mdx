## Getting Started: Progression and Factions

When you first spawn into the world, your first major decision is choosing a side: Good or Evil. While this choice impacts your starting location and the NPCs you interact with, the general progression remains remarkably similar. The core of the game revolves around level-based quests.

To level up efficiently, you should always visit the Quest NPC corresponding to your current level. By using the "Track Quest" feature, you can easily find your way to the next objective. Currently, the level cap has been raised past 3,000, meaning there is plenty of room for growth.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize main story quests over generic mob farming, as they provide significantly higher experience points and often lead you to essential NPCs for magic and gear.

## The Race Tier List and Stats

Your race is one of the most important factors in your character build. While everyone starts as a Human, you can use spins at the Trait Gacha NPC to change your race. Each race offers unique buffs that can drastically alter your effectiveness in PVP and PVE.

| Race | Rarity | Primary Buffs | Best For |

The **Devil race** remains the most sought-after due to its lifesteal capabilities, allowing you to sustain yourself during long boss encounters or intense raids. If you are focused on raw speed, the **Dark Elf** is an excellent alternative for those who rely on melee-magic combos.

## Grimoires and Magic Systems

Grimoires are the heart of your power in Clover Origins. You can obtain your magic by rolling at the Grimoire NPC. Each grimoire has different rarities and scaling properties. It is crucial to understand that while most magic scales with your **Magic** stat, there are notable exceptions.

| Grimoire | Rarity | Scaling Stat | Special Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lightning** | Uncommon | Magic | High speed, stun potential |
| **Wind** | Legendary | Magic | Includes a transformation Mode |
| **Anti-Magic** | Legendary | **Strength** | High damage, awakening mode |
| **Fire** | Common | Magic | Burn damage over time |

> **Warning:** If you roll Anti-Magic, do not invest points into the Magic stat! Anti-Magic scales exclusively with **Strength**, making it a unique physical-based "magic" style.

## Mobility and Utility: Brooms and Traits

Movement is key to exploring the vast map. Once you reach level 100, you can undertake a mini-questline to unlock your broom. By pressing "P," you can summon your broom and fly, which is essential for reaching high-altitude areas like the Lich Boss raid.

In addition to your race and grimoire, you can roll for **Traits**. Traits provide passive bonuses that are separate from your race. Most traits are unique, so finding a combination that complements your grimoire is the goal of any high-level player. For example, a trait that boosts mana regeneration is perfect for an Elf using Wind magic.

## Endgame Raids and Boss Encounters

To truly reach the pinnacle of power, you must participate in raids. These encounters drop rare materials required for "awakenings," which unlock the full potential of your grimoire.

### The Wolf Raid
Located in the **Lost Clover Valley**, the Wolf Raid is a timed event that only occurs during a **Full Moon**. You can find the entrance near the Mana Sense NPC. This raid is excellent for intermediate players looking to test their builds.

### The Lich Boss Raid
The Lich Boss is the current pinnacle of PVE challenge. To find him:
1. Head to the Grimoire Gacha area.
2. Fly up to the massive skull floating in the sky.
3. Enter the raid portal.

The Lich Boss has a chance to drop the **Orb**, a critical accessory used for the **Anti-Magic Awakening**. While Wind awakening is not currently in the game as of early 2026, the Lich remains the best source for high-tier loot and accessories.

## Weapons and Equipment

While magic is your primary tool, weapons provide additional movesets. The **Gun** is a standard secondary with two basic moves, but the **Imperial Sword** is the premium choice. Costing 1,000 Robux, the Imperial Sword offers high-damage AOE attacks that can clear mobs instantly. If you are a free-to-play player, keep an eye on official [Roblox](https://www.roblox.com) group updates, as the developers often hint at new farmable weapons in future patches.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more spins for races and grimoires in Clover Origins?**

A: Spins can be obtained by completing specific daily quests, using promotional codes released by the developers, or purchasing them with Robux in the in-game shop. This **clover origins roblox wiki** recommends saving your spins until you are at least level 500 to ensure you have a solid foundation before min-maxing.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up to 3,000?**

A: The fastest method is to follow the quest tracker religiously. Once you hit level 1,000, focus on the "Elite" quests and participate in the Wolf Raid during full moons for massive XP drops.

**Q: Can I change my side from Good to Evil later?**

A: Currently, your faction choice is permanent for that character slot. If you wish to experience the other side's story and unique NPCs, you will need to create a new character or use a faction reset item if available in the shop.

**Q: Where do I find the Anti-Magic Orb?**

A: The Orb is a rare drop from the Lich Boss raid located inside the giant skull. It is a mandatory item if you wish to unlock the "Awakened" mode for the Anti-Magic grimoire.
